To Stay or To Go

You have decided to wait and see if anyone will return. As you wait, you notice food sitting on the table. Too hungry to resort to another option, you take a bite of the apple. You feel fine, so you think the stranger does not mean to harm you. After a couple of hours, an old man enters the cabin. He lives here, and he is very nice. He tends to your wounds and provides you with your long-needed nutrition. You live with him for a couple of days, and one day he sets off to go hunting. You think about how he treats you, and approach the next decision.
